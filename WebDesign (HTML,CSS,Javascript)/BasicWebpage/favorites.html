Poker Games

I love to play poker games specifically Texas Hold'em and sometimes Omaha. Fun fact: I worked at the Grand Falls Casino and Golf Resort as a Poker Dealer for a couple years after high school. Gambling can be quite addictive though, so if you do come to play make sure not to bring more than your willing to spend, and be ready to leave when the cards don't go your way!

Tabletop Games

I also really like tabletop games, which basically comprises of most board games and TCGs (Trading Card Games). My favorite TCG is Magic, the Gathering, but when I was younger I played Pokemon and Yugioh too. A good board game for the upcoming holiday Halloween is called Betrayal at the House on the Hill. In that game you and the other team members work together to figure out what is haunting the house on the hill until it turns out that one player was secretly a traitor and is trying to kill all the other players! It is like playing a game based on horror movies.
